About Orion Hotel

Hotel Orion is a luxury hotel blending elegant modern design with authentic Japanese touches, offering a truly unique stay experience. Situated in a cityscape of towering skyscrapers and a breathtaking bay view, the hotel features top-notch amenities, including minimalist-designed rooms, a Japanese-inspired restaurant, and an onsen-style spa. With three branches located in Jakarta, Bali, and Yogyakarta, Hotel Orion ensures exceptional service and unforgettable stays at every location.

The Hotel Orion logo is inspired by the rising sun, a symbol of new beginnings and vitality, and a renowned emblem of Japan as the "Land of the Rising Sun." The design features a perfect circle representing the sun, radiating gentle beams with sleek, modern lines that embody the fusion of traditional Japanese culture and contemporary luxury. Warm red tones combined with elegant gold accents symbolize warmth, sophistication, and premium quality—key elements of Hotel Orion’s identity.
